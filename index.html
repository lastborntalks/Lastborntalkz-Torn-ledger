How to use Torn Ledger

Getting started

Paste your Torn API key in the Ledger tab and tap Save Key. No full access key needed. Tap "Create the right key for me on Torn" and it opens Torn's own key page with only the required permissions already selected.

Navigating the app

Use the tabs at the top or the icons at the bottom to switch between pages. Ledger shows your transaction history. Financial Health shows your complete money picture across trading, mugging, jobs, rehab, travel, and casino. Settings has appearance options and data tools.

Syncing your data

Tap "Sync Now" in the Ledger tab to pull recent activity. The very first time you use a key, it automatically pulls the last 6 months in the background. This can take several minutes for active accounts. Do not close the tab while it runs.

Loading full history

Tap "Load everything since account creation" for your complete history from day one. For veteran accounts this takes time. You'll see a warning first, and if it stops early it will tell you exactly where it stopped so you can continue from that point.

Reading the ledger

Each row is a real transaction. Tags in the Unit Price column tell you when adjustments were made: "-5% fee" means the Item Market seller fee was deducted, "+travel" means the flight cost was folded into that item's cost basis. "no buy record" usually means the item came from a crime or was already in your inventory before you started using the ledger.

Financial Health page

This page answers "Am I actually making money?" by combining trading profit with all other tracked income and expenses. Smart insights at the bottom are generated automatically from your real numbers, not hardcoded guesses.

Unmatched logs

After syncing, scroll to the bottom of the Ledger page and expand "Unmatched log entries." If anything is listed, copy it and send it to Lastborntalkz [4283156] in game. That is how the system gets taught to handle new log types and improves accuracy for everyone.

All time view

Switching to "All time" in any section can take a moment with a large history. It will ask you to confirm first. Switching back to it a second time is instant, since it uses a cached version.

⚠ Wipe and Full Resync

Found in Settings. This permanently deletes your local ledger and re-pulls everything fresh. Only use it if something looks badly wrong and a normal sync hasn't fixed it.

Privacy

All data, including your API key, your ledger, and your history, is stored only in your own browser. Nothing is sent to any server. The creator has no access to your data.

ABOUT
Torn Ledger is a free financial tracking tool for Torn City, built by Lastborntalkz [4283156].

It pulls data from your own Torn API key and calculates real profit and loss across every type of transaction, including bazaar sales, item market sales, player trades, and foreign purchases. All data is stored only in your own browser and is never sent to any server.

No full access key is required. The site generates the exact custom key it needs when you tap "Create the right key for me on Torn."

If you find a bug, encounter an unmatched log entry, or have a suggestion, reach out to Lastborntalkz [4283156] in game.
