How to use Torn Ledger

Getting started

Paste your Torn API key above and tap Save Key. Don't have the right kind of key? Tap "Create the right key for me on Torn" — it opens Torn's own page with the correct permissions already selected. You never need a full-access key.

Syncing

Tap "Sync Now" to pull your recent activity. The very first time you use a key, it automatically pulls your last 6 months in the background too — this can take a few minutes for an active account, so don't close the tab.

Loading your full history

Use "Load everything since account creation" to pull your entire trading history from day one. For very active accounts this can take a while — you'll see a warning before it starts, and it'll tell you honestly if it stops early so you can continue from where it left off.

Reading the ledger

Each row shows a real buy or sell. "no buy record" means the item was likely obtained another way (like a crime reward), not necessarily a mistake. Small tags like "-5% fee" or "+travel" show when a cost has been adjusted for Item Market fees or flight costs.

Unmatched logs

Anything the system doesn't recognize yet shows up under "Unmatched log entries" near the bottom. Please share these so the ledger can be taught what they mean.

All time views

Switching to "All time" can take a moment to load if you have a lot of history — that's normal processing, not a fault. Switching back to it again afterward should be instant.

Wipe & Resync

This permanently erases your local ledger and re-pulls everything fresh from Torn. Only use this if something looks broken and a normal sync hasn't fixed it.

Foreign area codes

The system can't know which country an area number means until you tell it once — enter the area number and country name after your first trip there, and it'll remember automatically after that.

Privacy

Everything — your key, your ledger, your history — is stored only in your own browser. Nobody else, including the creator, can see your data.
